Our menu

Traditional Japanise cuisine is popular all over the world, thanks to such a dishes as sushi and rolls. They are very diverse and in each region is unique.

Philadelphia roll

The Philadelphia roll is a type of sushi usually made with smoked salmon, cream cheese, and cucumber. They may also include other ingredients such as other types of fish, avocados, green onions, and sesame seeds.

Maki

Maki is a kind of sushi, one of the most famous Japanese dishes, which is served in the form of a roll. At the heart of this roll of rice soaked in rice vinegar, and complement it with various fillings. All this is wrapped in a dry sheet of nori algae - and you get the same rolls that are now eaten around the world.
